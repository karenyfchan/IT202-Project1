About

Japan

Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka, Yokohama, Nagoya, Kobe

Visit famous cities in Japan! Japan has a large variety of attractions. You can visit to the Fushimi Inari Shrine, hike up Mt. Fuji, try on Kimonos in Kyoto, explore the Himeji Castle, take pictures at the Golden Pavilion, go up the Tokyo Tower, and take a dip at the hot springs in Jigokundai Monkey Park.

  • Country: Japan
  • Capital: Tokyo
  • Cities: Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka, Yokohama, Nagoya, Kobe

Italy

Rome, Florence, Venice, Milan, Verona

Visit the most popular places in Italy! You can visit the Colosseum, go on a gondola ride through the Venice Canals, explore the city of Pompeii, take pictures with the Leaning Tower of Pisa, walk along Lake Como, walk along the Amalfi Coast, admire Florence Duomo Santa Maria del Fiore, browse the streets of Clinque Terre, and walk around Vatican City.

  • Country: Italy
  • Capital: Rome
  • Cities: Rome, Florence, Venice, Milan, Verona

Iceland

Reykjavik

Iceland has many unique tourist attractions that are hard to find anywhere else in the world. You can go whale watching, take a dip in the Blue Lagoon, admire the Strokkur Geysuir(Spectacular Geysers), experience the Northern Lights, see the volcano Landmannalaugar, walk around the Skaftafell Ice Cave, and admire the Dettifoss Waterfall.

  • Country: Iceland
  • Capital: Reykjavik
  • Cities: Reykjavik

Spain

Madrid, Barcelona, Seville

Exlore the beautiful country of Spain through these tourist attractions. You can visit the palace of Alhambra, walk around in the Mezquita of Cordoba, visit the Sagrada Familia Roman Catholic church, party on the Ibiza island, explore the medival city of Cuenca, admire the Aqueduct of Segovia, and relax on the La Concha beach.

  • Country: Spain
  • Capital: Madrid
  • Cities: Madrid, Barcelona, Seville

Brazil

Rio de Janeiro, Sao Paulo, Salvador

In Brazil you can visit the famous Christin the Redeemer statue, admire the Iguacu Falls, explore the Amazon Rainforest, experience a Rio Canival, see wildlife in the Pantanal, walk alone the Salvador Beaches, browse through the city of Olinda, go underwater in Bonito and visit the Teatro Amazonas(Amazon Theatre)

  • Country: Brazil
  • Capital: Rio de Janeiro
  • Cities: Rio de Janeiro, Sao Paulo, Salvador

South Africa

Cape Town, Johannesburg, Durban, Pretoria

South Africa is known for its beautifal landscape and their variety of wildlife. You can see elephants at Kruger National Park, cage dive with Greate White Sharks, browse the city of Cape Town, explore the Kalahari Gemsbok National Park, walke around the city of Stellenbosch, see the wonders of the Drakensberg Mountains, walk along the coast of The Garden Route, and take pictures at the Blyde River Canyon Natue Reserve.

  • Country: South Africa
  • Capital: Cape Town, Pretoria, Bloemfontein
  • Cities: Cape Town, Johannesburg, Durban, Pretoria
